BEST MOVIES

MY TOP 4 MOVIES OF ALL-TIME

1. THE WIND RISES

The Wind Rises was the highest-grossing Japanese film in Japan in 2013.
A lifelong love of flight inspires Japanese aviation engineer
Jiro Horikoshi (Hideaki Anno), whose storied career
includes the creation of the A6M World War II fighter plane.

2. MULAN

Acclaimed filmmaker Niki Caro brings the epic tale of China's legendary
warrior to life in Disney's "Mulan," in which a fearless young woman risks
everything out of love for her family and her country to become one of
the greatest warriors China has ever known.

3. THE CROODS

The Croods is a 2013 American computer-animated adventure comedy film produced by
DreamWorks Animation and distributed by 20th Century Fox.

4. RAYA AND THE LAST DRAGON

Raya and the Last Dragon (/ˈraɪ.ə/ RYE-ə) is a 2021 American
computer-animated fantasy action-adventure film produced by
Walt Disney Animation Studios and distributed by Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures.
